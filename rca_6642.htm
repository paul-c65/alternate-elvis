RCA 47  6642       Black Label – Dog On Top 

Side A.  Lawdy, Miss Clawdy

Side B.  Shake, Rattle And Roll

 

 

Matrix numbers: G2WW-1293-1S / G2WW-1294-1S (machine stamped)

Released in 1956 by RCA Records on 78 RPM as Lawdy Miss Clawdy / Shake Rattle and Roll.
Re-released by RCA Records on 45 RPM as Lawdy Miss Clawdy / Shake Rattle and Roll on the Gold Standard Series label.

Recording Dates

Lawdy Miss Clawdy - Recorded 31st January 1956, RCA Studio 1, New York

Shake, Rattle And Roll - Recorded 31st January 1956, RCA Studio 1, New York

 

Musicians:-

Elvis Presley  - Vocals, Guitar

Scotty Moore - Guitar

Bill Black - Bass

D.J. Fontana - Drums

Shorty Long – Piano

 Release Date:- August 31st 1956

 Chart Position:- US:-  / UK:-
