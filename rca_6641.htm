RCA 47  6641       Black Label – Dog On Top 

Side A.  Money Honey

Side B.  One-Sided Love Affair

 

 

Matrix numbers: G2WW-1232-4S A1 / G2WW-0210-4S A1 (machine stamped)

Released in 1956 by RCA Records on 78 RPM as Money Honey / One Sided Love Affair.
Re-released by RCA Records on 45 RPM as Money Honey / One Sided Love Affair on the Gold Standard Series label. 

Recording Dates

Money Honey - Recorded 10th January 1956, RCA Studio's, Nashville

One-Sided Love Affair - Recorded 30th January 1956, RCA Studio 1, New York 

 

Musicians:-

Elvis Presley  - Vocals, Guitar

Scotty Moore, Chet Atkins - Guitar

Bill Black - Bass

D.J. Fontana - Drums

Floyd Cramer, Shorty Long - Piano

Gordon Stoker, Ben Speer, Brock Speer – Vocals

 Release Date:- August 31st 1956

 Chart Position:- US:-  / UK:-
