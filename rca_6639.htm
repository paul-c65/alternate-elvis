RCA 47  6639        Black Label – Dog On Top
(this bootleg cover was made around about 1968)

Side A.  Trying To Get To You

Side B.  I Love You Because

Trying To Get To You was released in the UK with Lawdy, Miss Clawdy as the flip side in October 1957 on the His Master's Voice label, it has a purple label with silver lettering and reached number 16 on the UK chart, catalogue No. POP 408.

 

Matrix numbers: F2WW-8039-2S A1 / G2WW-1086-2S A1

Released in 1956 by RCA Records on 78 RPM as Tryin' To Get To You / I Love You Because.
Re-released by RCA Records on 45 RPM as Tryin' To Get To You / I Love You Because on the Gold Standard Series label.

Recording Dates

Trying To Get To You - Recorded 11th July 1955, Sun Studio, Memphis

I Love You Because - Recorded 5th July 1954, Sun Studios, Memphis

 

Musicians:-

Elvis Presley  - Vocals, Guitar

Scotty Moore - Guitar

Bill Black - Bass

Johnny Bernero – Drums

 Release Date:- August 31st 1956

 Chart Position:- US:-  / UK:- 16 (as B-side)
