EPA   5141                            Black Label – Dog On Top    Gold Standard Series label.

A Touch Of Gold, Volume 3

Side 1. Too Much / All Shook Up
Side 2. Don't Ask Me Why / Blue Moon Of Kentucky

 

Like volumes 1 & 2 this EP includes 3 RCA single tracks and 1 Sun track. This was a Gold Standard original release and some copies were printed on the rare maroon label..

This EP was also released in the UK, on the HMV label (RCX 7204)it did not chart.

Matrix numbers: K2PH-6305-1S A1 / K2PH-6306-1S A1 (machine stamped)

Released in 1969 with Orange label.
Matrix numbers: K2 PH6305-1S A2 / K2 PH6306-1S (machine stamped)

Recording Dates

Too Much - Recorded 2nd September 1956, Radio Recorders, Hollywood

All Shook Up - Recorded 12th January 1957, Radio Recorders, Hollywood

Don't Ask Me Why - Recorded 16th February 1958, Radio Recorders, Hollywood

Blue Moon Of Kentucky - Recorded July 7th 1954, Sun Studio, Memphis, Tennessee

EPA   4368                            Black Label – Dog On Top

 Follow That Dream

Side 1.  Follow That Dream / Angel
Side 2.  What A Wonderful Life / I’m Not The Marrying Kind

This EP made Billboard's Hot 100 list on May 12th 1962, it was number 15 and remained on the chart for ten weeks, "Follow That Dream" made the number 5 slot on Billboard's Easy Listening chart

June 1962 was the release date of this EP in the UK (RCX 211) it entered the UK charts on June 27th 1962, peaking at number 34 and it would reach the number 8 slot  in the UK charts.

Matrix numbers: N2PH-1603-1S A5 / N2PH-1604-1S A2

Also released in a orange label in 1968. N2PH-1603-1S A5 / N2PH-1604-1S A1

 Recording Dates

 Follow That Dream - Recorded July 2nd 1961, RCA Studio B, Nashville, Tennessee

Angel  - Recorded July 2nd 1961, RCA Studio B, Nashville, Tennessee

What A Wonderful Life - Recorded July 2nd 1961, RCA Studio B, Nashville, Tennessee

I’m Not The Marrying Kind - Recorded July 2nd 1961, RCA Studio B, Nashville, Tennessee

EPA   4371                            Black Label – Dog On Top

Kid Galahad

Side 1.  King Of The Whole Wide World / This Is Living / Riding The Rainbow

EPA   4382                            Black Label – Dog On Top

 Viva Las Vegas

Side 1.  If You Think I Don’t Need You / I Need Somebody To Lean On
Side 2.  C’mon Everybody / Today, Tomorrow And Forever

On July 4th 1964, "Viva Las Vegas"  EP was number 92 on the Hot 100 chart and it stayed on the chart for only one week
