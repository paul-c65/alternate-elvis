RCA 47  9610                        Black Label – Dog On Side

Side A.  A Little Less Conversation

Side B.  Almost In Love

 "Almost In Love" appeared on Billboard's Hot 100 chart on September 28th 1968 at the number 69 position, and stayed on this chart for four weeks.
Billboard cast "A Little Less Conversation" in the number 63 slot on October 12th 1968, remained on the chart for four weeks. November 29th 1968 was the release date of this 45 in the UK (RCA 1768), it didn’t chart in the UK charts.

Matrix numbers: WPKM-5766--1S A1 /WPKM-5767--1S A1

Also released with a yellow label, NOT FOR SALE.

Re-released by RCA Records on 45 RPM as A Little Less Conversation / Almost In Love on the Gold Standard Series label.

 Recording Dates

A Little Less Conversation  - Recorded March 7th 1968, Western Recorders, Hollywood

Almost In Love - Recorded  March 7th 1968, Western Recorders, Hollywood
