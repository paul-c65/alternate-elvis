EPB   1254                             Black Label – Dog On Top

 Elvis Presley

Side 1.  Blue Suede Shoes / I'm Counting On You
Side 2.  I Got A Woman / One-Sided Love Affair
Side 3. Tutti Frutti / Trying To Get To You
Side 4. I'm Gonna Sit Right Down And Cry (over you) / I'll Never Let You Go (little darlin')

This EP is very rare and was available with eight songs. Another version RCA SP 23 featured twelve songs, this is the only Elvis double EP released to the public.

Matrix numbers: G2WH-1850-5S A1 / G2WH-1851-6S A5 / G2WH-1852-6S B2 / G2WH-1853-5S

 Recording Dates

Blue Suede Shoes - Recorded January 30th 1956, RCA Studio 1, New York

I'm Counting On You - Recorded January 11th 1956, RCA Studios, Nashville

I Got A Woman - Recorded January 10th 1956, RCA Studios, Nashville

One-Sided Love Affair - Recorded January 30th 1956, RCA Studio 1, New York

Tutti Frutti - Recorded January 31st 1956, RCA Studio 1, New York

Trying To Get To You - Recorded July 21st 1955, Sun Studio, Memphis

I'm Gonna Sit Right Down And Cry (over you) - Recorded January 31st 1956, RCA Studio 1, New York

I'll Never Let You Go (little darlin) - Recorded Between 12th & 16th September 1954, Sun Studio, Memphis

EPA   4006                            Black Label – Dog On Top

Love Me Tender

Side 1.  Love Me Tender / Let Me
Side 2.  Poor Boy / We’re Gonna Move

Released November 1st 1956 this EP was Placed on Billboard’s Hot 100 singles chart at number 35 (Poor Boy), and number 9 on Billboard’s EP chart. This EP included all four songs from the soundtrack of Elvis’ first movie, “Love Me Tender”.


Some copies have the titles at the top of the front cover in black print on white background, while others have white print on black background. The later pressings from 1965 and 1968 don’t show the titles at all. Some rare copies were printed without the dog Nipper on the labels.

Released in the UK on the HMV label (7EG 8199)  in January 1957, it did not enter the charts.

Matrix numbers: G2WH-7523-1S A3 / G2WH-7524-1S A4

 Released in 1968 with Orange label.
Matrix numbers: G2 WH7523-1S B6 / G2 WH7524--8S A2

Recording Dates

Love Me Tender - Recorded August 1956, Fox Stage 1, Hollywood

Let Me - Recorded September 5th 1956, Fox Stage 1, Hollywood

Poor Boy - Recorded August 1956, Fox Stage 1, Hollywood

 We’re Gonna Move - Recorded August 1956, Fox Stage 1, Hollywood
