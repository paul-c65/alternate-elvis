RCA 47  8780                        Black Label – Dog On Side

Side A.  Frankie And Johnny

Side B.  Please Don’t Stop Loving Me

"Frankie And Johnny" appeared on Billboard's Hot 100 chart on March 1st 1966 at the number 25 position, and stayed on this chart for eight weeks.
"Please Don't Stop Loving Me" remained on the chart for the same amount of weeks after entering at number 45 on the same day. April 7th 1966 was the release date of this 45 in the UK (RCA 1509), it would reach the number 21 slot on the chart.

Please Don’t Stop Loving Me - Recorded May 13th 1965, Radio Recorders, Hollywood
