RCA 47  8307                        Black Label – Dog On Top

RCA 47  0720                        Black Label – Dog On Top

Side A.  Blue Christmas

Side B.  Wooden Heart

  Although “Blue Christmas” never made Billboard’s Hot 100 chart, it did appeared on their special Christmas charts for 12 seasons between 1964 and 1985. It was re-released with another flip in 1965, with “Santa Claus Is Back In Town” as the A-side and that single was available for a total of 20 years. RCA released this single in the UK charts in November 1964 (RCA 1430), with the B-side “White Christmas”,  it reached the number 11 position on the chart.

Matrix numbers: H2PW-5525--3S A2 / L2PW-3681--5S A1

Recording Dates

Blue Christmas - Recorded September 5th 1957, Radio Recorders, Hollywood

 Wooden Heart - Recorded April 28th 1960, RCA Studios, Hollywood

RCA 47  8740                        Black Label – Dog On Side

 Side A.  Tell Me Why

Side B.  Blue River

On January 1st 1966, both "Tell Me Why" & "Blue River" entered Billboard's Hot 100 chart, "Tell Me Why" was at number 33, and remaining on the chart for seven weeks, and "Blue River"  was number 95 and it remained on the chart for only one week.
