RCA 47  6640       Black Label – Dog On Top

Side A.  Blue Moon

Side B.  Just Because

Blue Moon was released under the His Master's Voice in the UK with the flip side I Don't Care If The Sun Don't Shine, it reached the number 9 slot in November 1956, purple label with gold lettering and later with silver lettering, catalogue No. POP 272.

 

Matrix numbers: F2WW-8117-2S A4 / F2WW-8118-2S A2

Released in 1956 by RCA Records on 78 RPM as Blue Moon / Just Because.
Re-released by RCA Records on 45 RPM as Blue Moon / Just Because on the Gold Standard Series label.

Recording Dates

Just Because - Recorded September 1954, Sun Studios, Memphis

Blue Moon - Recorded 19th August 1954, Sun Studio, Memphis

 

Musicians:-

Elvis Presley  - Vocals, Guitar

Scotty Moore - Guitar

Bill Black - Bass

Johnny Bernero – Drums

Release Date:- August 31st 1956

 Chart Position:- US:-  / UK:- 9
